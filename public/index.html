About us

TC Jones Groundworks is a deep drainage specialist established in 1968, founded by Chris Jones working for and alongside some of the industry’s leading water and sewer suppliers, becoming one of the water industry’s top sub-contractors in the UK today.

In the last 54 years with an exemplary health and safety record, delivering quality and excellence on schemes near and far. TC Jones has focused on installing, repairing, delivering and constructing sewers, water mains, shafts and pump stations of all varying sizes on projects and infrastructure schemes ranging anywhere from 50 working man hours up to 190,000 working man hours. All delivered to the highest of standards finding the best solution for each individual project.

As a south coast-based sub-contractor, our catchment area covers jobs and contracts in and around Hampshire, Wiltshire, Sussex, Kent, Dorset, Somerset, London, Greater London, and all the surrounding areas.
